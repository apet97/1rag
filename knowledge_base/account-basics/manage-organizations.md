# Manage Organization

Organization serves as a foundation for your collaboration in the CAKE.com ecosystem. It helps you structure and manage projects, tasks, and team communication in a systematic and efficient manner. 

Organization can be managed by organization owner and admin.

## Rename organization

If you’d like to redefine your organization’s identity or adapt to evolving project needs, you can rename your Organization following these steps:

1. Click on **Organization** in the left sidebar
2. Click on **Edit** next to the **Organization name**
3. Type new Organization name
4. Click **Save changes** to complete the action

## Manage organization members

To learn more about user management inside your organization, check out [this article](https://cake.com/help/administration/manage-user-account/).

## Create new organization

You can create a new CAKE.com organization via the link provided in this Help Center.

Here’s how:

1. Make sure you’re logged in to your CAKE.com account  
**If you’re not logged in, you’ll be asked to sign in first. After logging in, you’ll be automatically redirected to the organization setup page.**
2. Click this link: [https://account.cake.com/create\-organization](https://account.cake.com/create-organization)
3. Enter your organization name and choose the product you want to use
4. Submit the form to complete the setup

Once done, you’ll be taken directly to your new organization’s homepage with a confirmation message: “You’ve successfully created your new organization.”

If you change your mind at any point, you can exit the process and return to your account and the existing organization.

## Delete organization

If you no longer need your organization, you can delete it at any time. For detailed instructions on how to do this, check out [this](https://cake.com/help/account-basics/delete-organizations/) article.

Any changes you make to your organization will be visible across all the workspaces under the given organization and may have impact on all of the workspaces (e.g. deleting organization).

## Manage subscription

Organization owner can manage subscription and billing details. Managing subscription includes editing billing information, updating payment methods, upgrading or downgrading Workspaces’ subscription plan, as well as accessing your billing history.

To access the billing section:

1. Log into your CAKE.com Account
2. Navigate to the **Subscriptions** page from your account dashboard

![](https://cake.com/help/wp-content/uploads/2024/12/Screenshot-2024-12-30-at-11.20.17.png)
Check out [this article](https://cake.com/help/billing/subscription-management/) for more information on managing subscriptions.
