# How to uninstall Clockify mobile apps (Android and iOS)

1 min read

If you’re experiencing issues or need to reinstall the app for troubleshooting purposes, uninstalling and reinstalling the Clockify mobile app is a quick way to reset things. Here’s how to do it depending on your device. 

## Uninstall Clockify on Android [\#](#uninstall-clockify-on-android)

1. Locate the Clockify app icon on your home screen
2. Tap and hold the app icon
3. Select Uninstall
4. Confirm your decision

![](https://clockify.me/help/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Screenshot-2025-06-27-at-09.08.28-487x1024.png)
Alternatively: 

* Go to Settings \> Apps \> Clockify \> Tap Uninstall

## Uninstall Clockify on iOS [\#](#uninstall-clockify-on-ios)

1. Find the Clockify app icon on your home screen
2. Tap and hold the app icon until a menu appears
3. Tap Remove App
4. Select Delete App and confirm

![](https://clockify.me/help/wp-content/uploads/2025/06/Screenshot-2025-06-27-at-09.07.47-502x1024.png)
Alternatively:

* Go to Settings \> General \> iPhone Storage \> Find and tap Clockify \> Tap Delete App
