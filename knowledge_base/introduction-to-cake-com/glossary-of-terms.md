# C

#### [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Account

[CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Account is an account that is created when you register at any of CAKE.com products and join/create a [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) organization. You cannot access any of [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) apps without a [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Account. A single user account may belong to multiple Organizations.

#### [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Bundle

[CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Bundle is a comprehensive package that offers access to Enterprise subscription plans of all three essential productivity solutions: Clockify, Pumble, and Plaky.

#### [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Productivity Suite

CAKE.com Producitivity Suite is a cohesive set of productivity solutions designed to simplify and streamline your workflow. At its core, CAKE.com Suite comprises three distinct yet interconnected apps: Clockify, Pumble and Plaky.

#### [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) user

CAKE.com user is any person that owns [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Account, regardless of their role in [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) organization (owner, admin, member).

# D

#### Destination organization

The destination organization is the one where the user transfers the workspace.

# O

#### Organization

[CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) organization connects your team with the CAKE.com products you use, resembling a real\-world company structure. All workspaces (Clockify, Pumble, Plaky) created within it are a part of an organization, and you can create more as needed. Each organization has an Owner and all users can be managed at the organization level, so that there is a smooth coordination across different teams and tools.

#### Organization member

Member is a type of user within CAKE.com organization. Members have permissions limited to modifying content within the workspace they are a part of and their own [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) Account.

#### Organization Owner

Owner is the highest level role within the CAKE.com organization, and each organization can have only one owner. Owner has the authority to manage the organization, including all the organization settings, users, workspaces and subscriptions.

#### Origin organization

Origin organization is the organization from which the user intends to transfer the workspace.

# W

#### Workspace

Workspace is a dedicated digital environment where a team collaborates on tasks, projects, or communication. Within it, members can organize activities tied to their goals—whether it’s tracking time (Clockify), coordinating project tasks (Plaky), or facilitating team communication (Pumble). Each [CAKE.com](http://cake.com/) organization must have at least one workspace with one product, and switching between workspaces is simple.

#### Workspace Admin

Workspace admin is a user role available in Pumble, Plaky and Clockify. They are assigned by the organization owner and can help manage the app.They can create new workspace within the organization and edit existing ones (in which they have admin role), view and edit workspace data, and perform some user management actions within the workspace where they have admin role.
